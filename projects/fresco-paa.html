FRESCO: The Paschen-Œ± Star-forming Sequence at Cosmic Noon

Publication: ApJ (2024)

This work presents early results from the JWST First Reionization Epoch Spectroscopically Complete Observations (FRESCO) survey, focusing on galaxy star formation at 1.0 < z < 1.7. Using the Paschen-Œ± emission line, which is less sensitive to dust than HŒ± or UV indicators, we study the star-forming sequence with unprecedented clarity.
